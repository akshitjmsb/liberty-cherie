Brand Story

Liberty Chérie is a French-inspired fashion brand that celebrates romantic femininity with a playful, modern twist. Our name combines "Liberty" — representing freedom, confidence, and self-expression — with "Chérie" — the French term of endearment meaning "darling" or "beloved."

Inspired by the timeless beauty of vintage floral patterns and the effortless elegance of French style, we create pieces that make every woman feel cherished and free to express her unique personality.

Brand Elements

Floral Pattern

The signature Liberty Chérie floral pattern features romantic roses, delicate wildflowers, and botanical foliage. This pattern should be used as:

  • Background textures and borders
  • Packaging design elements
  • Social media decorative frames
  • Product tags and labels

Photography Style

Brand photography should evoke a romantic, whimsical mood with:

  • Soft, natural lighting with warm tones
  • Floral and botanical props
  • Vintage or French-inspired settings
  • Models that embody confidence and joy
